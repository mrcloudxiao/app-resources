Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025.10.14

This Privacy Policy applies to the Space Dodge X mobile application (the "App"). Space Dodge X is a casual arcade game. This document explains what information the App does — and does not — collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

  • No tracking: The App does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and it does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission.
  • No account & no personal data: We do not ask you to register, and we do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • No payments: This version contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
  • No Game Center: Leaderboards/Game Center are not enabled in this version.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data. The App does not ask for your name, email, phone number, precise location, advertising identifiers, or other data that can identify you.

Local gameplay data: Basic gameplay information (for example, your best survival time) is stored only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Diagnostics (optional): Apple may provide us with aggregated and anonymized crash logs and usage analytics through App Store Connect. These reports do not identify you and are used solely to improve stability and performance.

Data Tracking

None. The App does not use tracking technologies to follow your activity across other companies' apps or websites. The App does not access the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and does not display personalized advertising.

Third-Party Services

This version of the App does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, social logins, analytics SDKs that profile users, or external data collection services. If we add optional services in a future update, we will disclose them here and on the App Store product page, and we will respect Apple's privacy requirements.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly address the matter.

Data Retention & Sharing

We do not maintain servers for player accounts. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Local gameplay data stays on your device.

Your Choices

  • You may delete the App at any time to remove all locally stored gameplay data.
  • You can manage Apple diagnostics sharing in iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be available within the App or on our website, with the "Last updated" date adjusted accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mrcloudxiao@gmail.com

App Store disclosure note: On the App Store product page, this version of Space Dodge X can be marked as "Data Not Collected" and "Not Used to Track You", consistent with the statements above. If future versions add ads, analytics, or Game Center, the disclosure will be updated accordingly.
