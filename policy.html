Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025.10.19

This Privacy Policy applies to the Space Dodge X mobile application (the "App"). Space Dodge X is a casual arcade game. This document explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

  • No tracking: The App does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and it does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission.
  • Game Center enabled: The App integrates Apple’s Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Participation is optional and managed through your Apple ID.
  • No account & no personal data: We do not collect or store personally identifiable information on our own servers.
  • No payments: This version contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or precise location.

Game Center data: When you sign in to Apple’s Game Center, your gameplay-related information (such as leaderboard scores, achievements, and Game Center username) is processed and stored by Apple in accordance with Apple’s privacy policy. We do not have access to your Apple ID, email address, or any identifying account information.

Local gameplay data: Basic gameplay information (e.g., best survival time) may be stored on your device and removed if you uninstall the App.

Diagnostics (optional): Apple may provide us with aggregated and anonymized crash logs and usage analytics via App Store Connect. These do not identify you and are used solely to improve performance.

Data Tracking

None. The App does not use tracking technologies to follow your activity across other companies' apps or websites, does not access your advertising identifier (IDFA), and does not display personalized advertising.
